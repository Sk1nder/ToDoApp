Frequently Asked Questions

What is this website about?

This website is a task management platform designed to help you organize and track your daily tasks efficiently. Users can add, update, and view their tasks seamlessly.

How do I create a new task?

To create a new task, navigate to the "Add Task" section, fill in the required details such as title, description, deadline, and then click "Submit."

How do I view my to-do list?

You can view your to-do list by clicking on the "To-Do List" section. This will display all the tasks that are assigned to you and are currently incomplete.

How can I edit a task?

To edit a task, go to the "To-Do List" section, find the task you want to modify, and click on the title of task.

How do I mark a task as complete?

In the "To-Do List" section, find the task you want to mark as complete and click on the "Complete" button. The task will be updated to show that it is finished.

Why do I need to log in?

Logging in ensures that your tasks are securely stored and only accessible to you. It also allows you to keep track of your progress and manage tasks effectively.

What should I do if I encounter an error?

If you encounter any issues or errors, please contact our support team via the contact form on the website. We will assist you as soon as possible.
